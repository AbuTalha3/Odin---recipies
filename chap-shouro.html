chap shouro (A Local Dish of Hunza GB)

It is a local to GB traditional food item. where "chap" means "meat" and shouro means "bread". Mostly the beef or Yak meat is used and sereved in cold weather
of chilling winters better servet with "Qahwa" or Milk Tea "Chai"

ingredients

For filling:

  • 500 grm chicken mince
  • 500 grm chicken mince
  • 2 onions finely chopped
  • 1 tbsp finely crushed ginger garlic/ paste
  • 2 small tomatoes finely chopped
  • 1 tsp black pepper powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 1/2 tsp red chilli flakes(or adjust to taste)
  • 2 green chillies finely chopped
  • 2-3 tbsp freshly chopped coriander
  • 1/2 tsp wild cumin/black cumin

    • For dough:

  • 2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup wheat flour
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1-2 tbsp oil
  • Water to knead
  • 1 egg

    • For drizzle on top:

  • Desi ghee/ olive oil/ walnut oil/apricot oil

  1. Fill the chap shouro with mixture of mince meat and onions
  2. Heat up the hot plate (tawa / daow" Burushiski Lang")
  3. Add a chap shouro on the hot plate
  4. Bake from both sides until it is cooked well

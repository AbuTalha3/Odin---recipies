Dawdo (A Local GB Dish)

Dowdo is one of the famous and delicious soup in Pakistan. This is the specialty of Gilgit Baltistan.
Gilgit is not only famous because of its beauty but it is also very famous because of its delicious dishes. Dowdo is a thick creamy soup that has soft wheat noodles in it and it also has boiled mustard
leaves which give it a desi look and flavor. This is best to eat this in winter.
